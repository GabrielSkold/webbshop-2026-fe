ABOUT US

We are a team of Sole Searchers, working hard to build this site for you.

Sole Search is a sneaker drop platform built by students at Nackademin. Our mission is to make it easy to discover, track, and shop the most hyped sneaker releases — all in one place.

From limited collabs to everyday heat, we curate upcoming drops so you never miss a release. Built with passion for sneaker culture and a drive to keep improving.
